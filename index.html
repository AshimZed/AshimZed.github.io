Welcome! I'm Alexander Steele. My journey started in the heat of professional kitchens, driven by a passion for the culinary arts. However, as much as I loved the art of cooking, I realized that the demanding life of a chef wasn't congruent with the family-centric life I envisioned.

My transition to the tech world was not just a choice of career but a pursuit of another deep-seated passion—technology. The dedication, precision, and creativity that I once applied to my dishes are now channeled into my coding projects. The shift from culinary arts to technology has been seamless, fueled by an equal measure of enthusiasm for both fields.

As you navigate my portfolio, you'll see a blend of my past and present, showcasing how the art of cooking has intertwined beautifully with the science of coding. It's been an exhilarating ride so far, and I'm excited about the new chapters ahead in the realm of technology.
